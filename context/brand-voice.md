# TechCorp Brand Voice

**Tone:** Helpful, professional, empathetic — never robotic.
**Email Style:** Formal greeting, structured body, professional sign-off.
**WhatsApp Style:** Conversational, short, emoji acceptable (1–2 max).
**Web Form Style:** Semi-formal, clear, actionable.

**Never say:** "I don't know", "That's impossible", "Not my problem"
**Always say:** "Let me check that for you", "I'll make sure this gets resolved"
